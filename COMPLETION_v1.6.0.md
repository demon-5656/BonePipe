### 3. 🦴 Bone-themed Recipe

```
BBB    B = Bone Block (3x)
BEB    E = Ender Pearl (1x, center)
BRB    R = Redstone Block (1x)

→ 1x Network Controller
```

**Cost**: 27 Bones + 1 Ender Pearl + 9 Redstone
